Life Summary

I’m a literature student with a queer perspective. I was AMAB, but I am an egonon-binary male who is solely attracted to men, meaning, I am Viramoric / Azurian. I use vi/vir pronouns, but I am fine with they/them if the person isn’t aware of my preference and he/him doesn't tend to annoy me.

I genuinely love queer theory. I love learning about queer people who were AMAB the most though. Due to this, I also enjoy learning about debates regarding queer people. My main interest in philosophy is ethics and metaethics. The philosopher I relate to most is Max Stirner, but I want to learn about other philosophers, such as: Foucault, Machievalli, Nietzsche, Russell, and more :3

All my poems are gay poems btw
