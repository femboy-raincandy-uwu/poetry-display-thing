"Conflagration"

Rainbow :3

We found salvation's sweet creation
We took it through invitation's gentle persuasion
Such joyful celebration.

With much admiration's warm foundation
With little accusation's subtle evasion
Yet, there was alienation's cold desolation.

Our congregation's fervent expectation
Our roaring conflagration's wild sensation:
A faux salvation's bitter devastation.
