"I Am — A Fykie Dowf"

Rainbow :3

You claim I'm important,
You claim such wondrous things,
You claim you love me

Yet — Who I am you no care nor know
It is not joy,
But panic that you feel

You claim I'm important,
You claim such wondrous things,
You claim you love me

Yet — Who I am you no care nor know
It is not me you love,
But the words I say

You claim I'm important,
You claim such wondrous things,
You claim you love me

Yet — Who I am you no care nor know
It is not me you want —
I am but a fykie dowf
